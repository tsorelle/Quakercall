How we use your information

As an endorser of the Call, you will be listed on our website. Your name, city and state will be listed, but no other personal information will be displayed. See our endorser list for examples..

The information you provide to Quaker Call is used for future communication with you and to help us understand more about the constituency the Call to Action has reached.

By submitting this form, we assume that you have granted us permission add you to our email list which will be used to notify you of other events and resources for non-violent action. You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the link in any message your receive.

We will not share your personal information with any other organizations or individuals nor will we expose your personal information such as email address, phone number or postal address to anonymous visitors to our websites.
